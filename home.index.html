  • Project Concert

Project Concert is a web-based system created specifically for the Master of Science in Anesthesia (MSA) program. Its purpose is to track data related to the program and provide a user-friendly interface for MSA students to document their clinical experiences. The system allows students to record the precise start and end times of their total clinical day and conveniently schedule daily clinical evaluations.

The main goal of Project Concert is to streamline data management and ensure easy access and meticulous organization of clinical data for both students and administrators. By utilizing this cutting-edge platform, MSA students can navigate the intricate realm of clinical data effortlessly. The system aims to transform their educational journey into an elegant symphony of knowledge and progress.

Overall, Project Concert is designed to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of data tracking and management within the MSA program, making it easier for students and administrators to access and organize important information related to clinical experiences.
