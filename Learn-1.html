Welcome to the Project Concert video tutorial! This engaging video aims to guide students through the process of accessing and managing their accounts effectively. By following these steps, you will have no trouble navigating the platform and making necessary updates to your information. Let's get started! Step 1: Accessing the Project Concert Website To begin, visit the Project Concert website by clicking on the following link: Project Concert Website. This will take you to the login page. Step 2: Logging In and Changing Your Password Once you've arrived at the login page, enter your login credentials to access your account. After logging in, it is highly recommended that you change your password for security purposes. To change your password, simply click on the "Change Password" option. You will be prompted to enter a new password in the "New Password" text box. Then, re-enter the new password in the "Confirm New Password" text box. Finally, click on the "Change Password" button. If both passwords were entered accurately, your password will be successfully updated. Step 3: Updating Your Information In addition to changing your password, it is crucial to keep your information up to date in the student record. To do this, click on the "Information" tab. Here, you can view and edit your personal details as desired. Once you have made the necessary changes, remember to click on the "Save" button to update your information in the system. By following these straightforward steps, you can confidently access Project Concert, safeguard your account with a new password, and ensure your information is accurate and current. (Begley, 2020) Feel free to reach out if you have any further questions or need additional assistance. Enjoy your experience with Project Concert!
