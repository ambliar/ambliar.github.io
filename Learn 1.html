This video (Begley, 2023) is designed to provide students with the information needed to access Project Concert. In addition to changing their password and entering their information and demographics in accordance with program policy.

First, students will need to visit the Project Concert website, accessible here. Once logged in, students are advised to change their password. To change a system password, the user can click Change Password. The user will be prompted to enter a new password in the New Password text box and again in the Confirm New Password text box and click Change Password. If both passwords were entered accurately, the password will be changed.

In addition to changing their passwords, students are advised to update their information in the student record. To do this, click Information. Click on the tab to View/Edit. Complete the information in fields, as desired, and click Save (Begley, 2020).
